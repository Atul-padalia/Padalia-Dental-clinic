About Us

At Padalia's Dental Clinic, we are dedicated to our patients having the best and most comfortable dental experience. Warmth and hospitality are at the heart of our practice. We are happy to accommodate you with a relaxing waiting area and comfortable treatment. We believe you deserve only the best in oral health care.

Our Services

Cavity Filling

At our clinic, we use silver, ceramic and cosmetic fillings, depending on need and circumstance of the patient, in order to fill cavities.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth cleaning, along with whitening and bleaching is done with utmost care, for a smile that looks not just beautiful, but also healthy.

Ortho-Treatment

Teeth straightening treatments are performed at our clinic using invisible braces too, among other alternative methods.

Extraction Procedures

Simple and surgical tooth extractions including wisdom tooth extractions are carried out with proper sterilisation care.

Polishing

We also offer services to clean and polish teeth, and recommend our patients to do so every 6 months to avoid chances of infection and cavities.
